Sample cases:
First boy Second girl (A ^ Bc)
First girl Second boy (Ac ^ B)
First boy Second boy (A ^ B)***
First girl Second girl (Ac ^ Bc) (excluded since at least one of them is a boy)

So, there are 3 possibilities and only the third is the probability of both bein a boy.

Answer: 1/3
